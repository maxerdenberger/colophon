Colophon — A directory of independent senior creatives.

Colophon is a directory of senior independent creatives — directors, designers, copywriters, and strategists who have left the agency system to work directly with the people who hire them. Access is by membership. Each name is personally known to the editors.

Disciplines

  • creative direction
  • art direction
  • design (brand, digital, product)
  • copywriting
  • brand strategy
  • design leadership

Membership

  • day — $29
  • week — $79
  • month — $249

On admission

Inclusion is by referral and editor review. Applicants are asked to name three peers whose work they would personally vouch for, and one client, brand, or studio they believe should have access to the directory. Decisions are issued quarterly.
