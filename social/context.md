# Product context for the board

Read this on first call of a session. For deeper detail see `HANDOFF.md` and `colophon-handoff.md` in the repo root.

## What Colophon is

A directory of senior independent creatives. Buyers pay for a `look` pass that unlocks contact info on the bench. Talent applies for inclusion and is vetted manually. Concierge is a $199 fast-path: buyer pays, fills a brief, gets five vetted names back within hours.

**URL:** `colophon.contact`

## Who buys

Marketing leads, founders, design directors, agency producers — people who need to hire a senior independent and are tired of the agency markup and roster shuffle. The pitch is *small on purpose · real-time · no agency in the middle*.

## What we sell

| Offer | Where in product | What you get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day / week / month pass | Tab 02 "build a bench" | Filtered access to the bench with contact info |
| Concierge | Tab 03 "concierge" | $199 brief in → 5 names within hours |
| Apply | Tab 04 "join the bench" | Talent applies for inclusion (vetted manually) |
| Access | Tab 05 "access" | Returning buyers use their code |

## Conversion surfaces

- **`/look`** — the wizard / filter view. Primary destination for B2B/lead-gen posts.
- **`/concierge`** — high-intent destination, $199 unit economics.
- **`/`** — the homepage with the bench teaser is fine for atmospheric posts.
