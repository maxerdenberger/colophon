# Banned words and banned moves

Hard nos. The Chair greps for these before any post queues.

## Banned words

- **leverage** (verb or noun)
- **synergy / synergistic**
- **rockstar / ninja / guru / wizard**
- **ecosystem**
- **best-in-class**
- **passionate**
- **disrupt / disruptor**
- **journey** (in the marketing sense)
- **unlock** (overused; sometimes OK in product UI, never in copy)
- **curated** (every competitor says this; show, don't tell)
- **seamless**
- **empower**
- **game-changer / game-changing**

## Banned moves

- Capitalizing for emphasis: NOT "Available NOW."
- Using emoji for tone (one orange dot in the IG visual = the brand mark, not emoji)
- Calling out a competitor by name in a post (private comparison in concierge follow-ups is fine)
- "Limited time" / "Last chance" / urgency manufactured rather than real
- Asking "what do you think?" or any post that begs comments
- Tagging real founders/companies as social proof without written consent
- Engagement-bait questions ("Round 1: which is worse — bad brief or bad timing?")
- Threads / carousels where each beat re-pitches the product

## When the line is fuzzy

The Chair's default: kill it. If we're debating whether a word belongs, the answer is no.
